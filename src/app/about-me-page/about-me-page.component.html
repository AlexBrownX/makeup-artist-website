About me

I am Jana, a professional freelance makeup artist based in London and the South East. I started my fashion business in 2008 and studied makeup at London College of Fashion. I have also completed many short courses with Illamasqua, a beauty range.

A very creative and artistic individual, I have been passionate about makeup and beauty since a young age. I remember helping my girlfriends with their makeup, hair and even style their outfits since I was a teenager. I really enjoy experimenting with new looks and styles. To compliment my wedding makeup passion, I also have experience with beauty, fashion, fitness, fetish, glamour, lingerie, pin up, and Halloween makeup.

My passion for wedding makeup blossomed when friends and family started asking me to do their makeup for them. This was both an honour and a privilege and allowed me to see exactly how a wedding is one of the most important and memorable days in a persons life. Women from all over the world want to look their best for their big day. Wedding makeup should give a bride confidence insuring she looks and feels like the very best version of herself. Being a part of your special day is a unique experience for me seeing all the stunning brides, gorgeous and happy on their special day, is now my biggest joy and achievement.

Why me?

I have a long and passionate love affair with makeup. It has become an important and essential part of my live. I am here to help make your special occasion memorable, completing your look with a touch of beauty and elegance. Whenever you are planning an intimate wedding or having an extravagant event with a large bridal party you can relax knowing you are in safe hands with me. The style of makeup I produce can be pure or alluring, but always glamorous and gorgeous. We will discuss your ideas and style and together we will find the perfect way to make you look and feel incredible.
